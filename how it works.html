How Digital Footprint Mirror Works

1. Collect Minimal Data

We only collect the necessary information from your signup details and browsing behavior with your permission. We respect your privacy and don’t store sensitive information.

2. Analyze Your Digital Behavior

Using advanced client-side scripts, we analyze your browsing patterns, visited sites, and search preferences to give you insights into your digital identity.

3. Generate a Score

Our system evaluates your footprint based on privacy exposure, security concerns, and online reputation. You’ll receive a detailed report and tips for improvement.

4. Suggest Improvements

You’ll get actionable advice to enhance your online privacy and reputation — such as changing passwords, limiting permissions, or updating your social presence.
